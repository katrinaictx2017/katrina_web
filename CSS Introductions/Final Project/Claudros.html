Claudros

Claudros is located North east of the map, above Sylvalum. The region is similar to earth's volcanic terrain. It's considerd as the most dangerous to explore because of the big pools of molten lava and it's rough weather conditions. It also contains old ruins of an ancient civilization. Chapter 10's plot mainly focuses on this region after it's been revealed that the Ganglion's home base takes place there.
