Sylvalum

Sylvalum is located approximately at the center of the map,
southwest of Claudros and above Primordia. Sylvalum region
is a mysterious place with it's white sand like surface and intruiging
column shaped mountain figures with alien-like glowing spheres of trees.
Chapter 9's story segement will take place in this area after a report
arrives of another "lifehold" spotted there.
