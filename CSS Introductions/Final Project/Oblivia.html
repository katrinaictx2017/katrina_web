Oblivia

Oblivia is located at the lower right side of the map, west of Primordia. It is a desert like region with beach-like sand and crystal clear rivers and a beautiful oasis waiting at the top of a waterfall. You'll see old ruins left over by an ancient alien race long long ago surrounded by gigantic ring-shaped waterpipe structures. Chapter 5 takes place here, The Protagonist and his team goes on a mission to check out the new alien forces that have landed in Oblivia.
